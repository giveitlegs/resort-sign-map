Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 8, 2026
Last Updated: February 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Resort Sign Mapper ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • User-Entered Data: Sign descriptions, location descriptions, contact information, and other details you enter into the App
  • Photographs: Images you capture using the App's camera functionality
  • Access Credentials: Access codes required to use the App

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when you document a sign (with your permission)
  • Device Information: Device identifiers, operating system version, and device type
  • Usage Data: App feature usage and interaction data
  • Timestamps: Date and time of data entries

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To document and catalog signage for property management purposes
  • To create reports and presentations from collected data
  • To sync data with authorized cloud services (Google Sheets, Google Slides)
  • To improve the App's features and user experience
  • To ensure authorized access to the App

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third-party services to provide App functionality:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Google Sheets Data storage and backup Sign data, location, timestamps
Google Slides Report generation Sign data, images, location
imgbb Image hosting Sign photographs
OpenStreetMap Map display Location coordinates (view only)

3.2 Organizational Sharing

Data collected through the App may be shared with your employer or the organization that authorized your access to the App.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored locally on your device and in authorized cloud services
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • Cloud data is protected by the respective service providers' security measures
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties

5. Data Retention

We retain collected data for as long as necessary to provide the Services and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Cloud-synced data is retained according to your organization's policies.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Location Permissions

You can enable or disable location services through your device settings. Note that disabling location services will affect the App's core functionality.

6.2 Camera Permissions

Camera access is required to capture sign photographs. You can manage this permission through your device settings.

6.3 Data Deletion

You can delete locally stored data by uninstalling the App. For cloud-synced data deletion requests, contact your organization administrator.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
